Cladding

Cladding serves as the building’s primary shield against external elements, delivering thermal insulation, weather protection, and structural stability, while enhancing its modern appearance.

Curtain Walling

Curtain walling plays a crucial role in maintaining environmental control, making it essential for facilities like data centres and pharmaceutical laboratories that demand precise internal conditions. Our lightweight aluminium systems, combined with high-efficiency glazed or opaque panels, are designed to resist wind, rain, and temperature changes—meeting the rigorous demands of mission-critical environments.

Façades & Rainscreen Systems

Featuring a robust exterior layer and a ventilated cavity, façades and rainscreen systems boost energy efficiency and support long-term performance—crucial for high-stakes environments where durability and reliability are imperative. Our expertise covers fibre cement, brick slips, ceramic and terracotta tiles, timber cladding, as well as zinc, copper, stainless steel, and aluminium cassettes.

Specialists in cladding, curtain walling and rainscreen systems across Europe.
